We welcome visitors at any time to our Sunday Services, held at 10:00 AM on the 1st and 3rd Sundays of every month. Please join us for a coffee hour following each service.

Directions
Harmony is located at 8639 Columbia Road in Maineville, Ohio. Our building is located on the campus of Lakeview United Church of Christ. Harmony's building is on the left and Lakeview's building is on the right as you are walking from the shared parking lot. Just outside our door there is a sign that says "Harmony, a Unitarian Universalist Community."

Parking
There is ample parking in the lot that we share with Lakeview United Church of Christ.
What to Wear
Wear whatever makes you comfortable. When you walk through our front doors, you’ll see everything from shorts and flip-flops, to business casual to kids in pink tutus or Spiderman costumes. We care less about what you’re wearing and more about making you feel welcome.
Children
Children ages three and older are invited to attend our Children's Religious Exploration (RE) classes. Additionally, childcare is provided for children ages three and younger. We understand some children may have difficulty separating from their parents in a new environment. Parents are welcome to join their children in RE classes to help ease any anxiety.

If you have any questions, please read our FAQs or contact us. We would love to hear from you!
