Harmony is committed to making a difference in the world through community service and social justice projects. We strive to choose service projects where kids can get involved. In fact, some of our service projects are 100% designed and run by kids! Harmony members are involved in many projects for both local and national organizations, including:

  • Supporting LGBT rights
  • Volunteering at Matthew 25: Ministries
  • Volunteering at The Hope Factory
  • Collecting donations for local food pantries and children's shelters
  • Volunteering at Granny's Garden School
  • Collecting donations for Nothing But Nets
  • Collecting pajamas and children's books for the Nationwide Pajama Program
  • Collecting donations to support disaster relief

For more information, or to find out how to get involved, contact us at info@harmonyuu.org.
