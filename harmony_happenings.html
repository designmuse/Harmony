Harmony offers a lot more than just Sunday services and children's RE classes. Here are some of the many ways to experience Harmony:

  • Family-friendly social gatherings: movie matinees, potlucks, Trunk-or-Treat, Santa's Workshop, etc.
  • Nonfiction book discussion groups
  • Circle Suppers: rotating small group dinners
  • Adult Religious Exploration: small group discussions
  • Youth group outings

Sunday, April 17 at 10:00 am - Sunday Service (Childcare Provided)
April 17th: “Finding Happiness in a Committed Life” by Erin Kotch How many times have you looked back on a busy week, month or even a whole year and felt like you never did anything fun? Of course there simply isn't time when demands come from so many sources: work, kids, spouses, parents, housework, holidays… the stress can be overwhelming. We look at all these commitments and see a need take some down-time, to "veg out" in order to recharge: the remedy is obvious, and completely wrong.
